================================================================================
                           KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY
================================================================================

1. OVERALL THINNING:
   - Mean elevation change: -1.03 m (per-glacier) / -2.42 m (pixel-weighted)
   - Median elevation change: -2.95 m
   - Thinning rate: -0.129 m/yr (per-glacier mean)

2. REGIONAL PATTERNS:
   - Bhutan: Highest thinning rate (-0.182 m/yr)
   - Arunachal Pradesh: -0.120 m/yr
   - Sikkim: Lowest thinning (-0.046 m/yr)

3. GLACIER COVERAGE:
   - 71.4% of glaciers show net thinning
   - 28.6% show net thickening or stable conditions

4. VOLUME LOSS:
   - Total: -12.40 km³ over ~8 years
   - Water equivalent: -11.16 km³
   - Annual rate: ~1.55 km³/yr

5. ALTITUDINAL PATTERN:
   - Maximum thinning at 4500-5500m elevation
   - High elevations (>6000m) show minimal change
